#  HR Attrition Analysis | Adecco India

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition at Adecco India to identify key factors driving employee turnover and provide actionable insights to improve retention.

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations as it impacts productivity, increases hiring costs, and affects team stability. This analysis focuses on understanding **why employees leave, who is most likely to leave, and what can be done to reduce attrition.**

---

##  Business Objective

* Identify key drivers of employee attrition
* Analyze high-risk employee segments
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve retention

##  Key Insights

###  Major Findings:

* Employees working overtime have **~3x higher attrition**
* Entry-level employees (**Job Level 1**) show the highest attrition (~26%)
* Attrition is highest in the **first year (~35%)**
* Low job satisfaction significantly increases attrition risk
* Sales department has the highest attrition among all departments

##  Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime to improve work-life balance
* Focus on onboarding and engagement in the first year
* Improve job satisfaction through feedback and recognition programs
* Provide better compensation and growth opportunities for entry-level employees

---

##  Conclusion

The analysis reveals that attrition is primarily driven by **workload, early employee experience, and job satisfaction**. Addressing these factors can significantly improve employee retention and organizational stability.
